Bipolar Disorder

In this video, we explore the basics of bipolar disorder and common misconceptions, including how mood lability - which is a rapid fluctuation in mood - is not bipolar disorder. Bipolar disorder is characterized by episodes of low or elevated mood that last for weeks to months.
