Goals Can Be Moved Between Quadrants

You don't have to abandon your current goals if they started in the wrong place. The introspective process itself can shift a goal's origin. When you start asking — What do I actually care about? Who do I care about? What obligations have I chosen to take seriously? What kind of person do I want to be? — you can begin anchoring existing goals to internal sources of motivationMotivation is not a feeling, a desire, or a behavior — it is the ability to hold one thought steadily in your mind for a prolonged period of time.Full definition →.

A goal that started as "I should exercise more" can become "I care about having the energy and health to show up fully for my family." The behavior might look similar. The fuel source is completely different.
