How Do I Find My Partner

In this video, we explore the question of how to find a partner. Since 2013, more people who get married found their partner online, and online relationships can be just as successful as those formed IRL. However, dating apps come with significant downsides: over 65% of Tinder users are already in relationships, about half use apps for emotional coping or entertainment rather than dating, and satisfaction on apps is inversely correlated with how interested you are in a relationship. We'll discuss what actually drives gut reactions on apps, including physical attractiveness, robustness or health, emotional stability, and sense of purpose, and why focusing on the fundamentals of human connection matters more than following arbitrary dating rules.
