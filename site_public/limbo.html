Action Items

  1. Notice what's holding you back in the present moment. When you imagine taking the leap, what stops you — and is part of it a hope about how things could turn out?
  2. Map the fantasy. What exactly are you picturing happening if things go well? Get specific about the dream you're protecting.
  3. Grieve it deliberately. Acknowledge to yourself that this outcome may never come to pass. Let yourself feel the loss of that possibility now, before any conversation happens.
  4. Then act. Once the dream isn't something you're desperately protecting, the conversation becomes far less catastrophic to attempt.
