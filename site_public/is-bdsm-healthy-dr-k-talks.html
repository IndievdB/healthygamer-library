How Psychiatry's View Has Changed

Psychiatric attitudes toward sexuality have shifted dramatically over time. Homosexuality was classified as a pathology in the DSM-3 (circa the 1970s). BDSM was similarly pathologized in the DSM-4. The DSM-5 moved toward acknowledging that milder forms of BDSM are not inherently disordered. The ICD-11 has also been updating its classifications.
