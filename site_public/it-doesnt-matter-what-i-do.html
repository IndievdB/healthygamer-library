It doesn't matter what I do.

As we lose hope in ourselves, our mind starts to tell us that it doesn't matter what we do. The odd thing is that while this may seem like an obstacle to overcome, this thought or feeling can be a lesson that we learned. In order to understand how to overcome this thought complex, we have to understand where it comes from, and what function it serves.
